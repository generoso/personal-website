Welcome to my website!

My name is Generoso Pagano and I am a software engineer, a guitarist and a world traveler.

Something I am doing now...

I am currently working as R&D engineer at Inria, a French national research institution focusing on computer science and applied mathematics, in Grenoble (France). I work on the research project SoC-Trace, in partnership with STMicroelectronics, Probayes, Magillem Design Services, and the LIG Laboratory.

I am an open source enthusiast and I collaborate with some open source projects (Framesoc, Lttng, Ocelotl).

Something I've done before...

I previously worked in Italy, my native country, at Evidence, a young and dynamic software company. Here I developed some modules of a flight simulator and I participated to the design and development of SMCube, a state machine editor and code generator.

I got my Master's Degree in Computer Engineering from the University of Pisa. During my Master's thesis, I designed an LTE-Advanced scheduling algorithm, which has been patented in collaboration with Telecom Italia.
